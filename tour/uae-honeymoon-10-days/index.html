UAE Luxury Honeymoon

Begin your unforgettable UAE honeymoon with a stay at Dubai's iconic 7 Star or 5 Star Hotel. DUBAI 10 DAYS LUXURY HONEYMOON PACKAGE

Upon arrival at Dubai International Airport, you will be greeted and escorted by a private chauffeur to your luxurious hotel in Dubai. Spend the rest of the day at leisure or explore nearby attractions at your convenience.

Meals:
Accommodation: 5-star Luxury Hotel
Transportation: Private vehicle

Enjoy a luxurious experience with a visit to the iconic Burj Al Arab, followed by a trip to the top of Burj Khalifa for breathtaking views of Dubai. Conclude the day with a magical water fountain show at Dubai Mall.

Meals: Breakfast
Accommodation: 5-star Luxury Hotel
Transportation: Private vehicle

Explore Dubai's cutting-edge attractions, starting with the Dubai Frame and Museum of the Future. In the afternoon, relax with a beach getaway experience provided by Waha Wonders.

Meals: Breakfast
Accommodation: 5-star Luxury Hotel
Transportation: Private vehicle

Spend the first half of the day resting or shopping, with a driver available at your disposal. In the afternoon, a makeup artist will arrive to prepare you for a glamorous photoshoot. Enjoy a luxurious photoshoot in a convertible car at various iconic spots around Dubai. End the day with a romantic date night at a unique restaurant.

Meals: Breakfast, Dinner
Accommodation: 5-star Luxury Hotel
Transportation: Private vehicle, Convertible Car

Spend the day at the Palm Jumeirah, exploring its luxury attractions. In the afternoon, enjoy an exhilarating desert safari, complete with dune bashing, camel rides, and a traditional Arabian dinner under the stars.

Meals: Breakfast, Dinner
Accommodation: 5-star Luxury Hotel
Transportation: Private vehicle, Desert Safari

Check out of your Dubai hotel and transfer to Abu Dhabi. Visit the thrilling Ferrari World for a high-speed adventure, followed by a visit to SeaWorld for a day of marine exploration and entertainment.

Meals: Breakfast
Accommodation: 5-star Luxury Hotel in Abu Dhabi
Transportation: Private vehicle

Explore the cultural wonders of Abu Dhabi, beginning with the majestic Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque. Continue with a guided city tour, visiting key landmarks and attractions in the capital.

Meals: Breakfast
Accommodation: 5-star Luxury Hotel in Abu Dhabi
Transportation: Private vehicle

Take a scenic long desert drive while capturing your journey. The day concludes with an exquisite dinner at a top-class desert restaurant, offering breathtaking views and a memorable dining experience.

Meals: Breakfast, Dinner
Accommodation: 5-star Luxury Hotel in Abu Dhabi
Transportation: Private vehicle

Return to Dubai in the morning and embark on a relaxing journey to Jebel Jais, the highest peak in the UAE. Spend the rest of the day at leisure, relaxing and enjoying the serene atmosphere.

Meals: Breakfast
Accommodation: 5-star Luxury Hotel in Dubai
Transportation: Private vehicle

Spend your final day shopping at Dubai's renowned malls or exploring any last-minute attractions before being transferred to the airport for your departure flight. Take with you unforgettable memories of your luxury honeymoon in Dubai and Abu Dhabi.

Meals: Breakfast
Accommodation:
Transportation: Private vehicle
